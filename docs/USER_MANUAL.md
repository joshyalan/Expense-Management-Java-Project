# User Manual: Expense Tracker Pro

Welcome to Expense Tracker Pro! This guide will walk you through setting up and using your new financial command center.

## 1. Getting Started

### Creating an Account
When you launch the app, you will see the Login Screen. Click **"Register here"**.
Provide a unique username, a secure password, and your email address. Once registered, log in to access your Dashboard.

### The Dashboard
Your Dashboard is your homepage. It displays:
- **Total Balance**: The sum of money across all your accounts.
- **Monthly Income & Expenses**: How much money entered and left your accounts this calendar month.
- **Health Score**: A dynamically calculated score (out of 100) based on your income-to-expense ratio.
- **AI Insights**: Automated tips and anomaly detection based on your recent spending.

## 2. Managing Accounts
Before you can log an expense, you need an Account (like a Wallet, Checking Account, or Credit Card).
1. Click **"Accounts"** on the left sidebar.
2. Click **"+ Add Account"**.
3. Name your account (e.g., "Chase Checking") and enter its current starting balance.

## 3. Categories
Categories keep your transactions organized. We provide default categories, but you can create your own.
1. Click **"Categories"** on the sidebar.
2. Here you can add custom Income or Expense categories with unique colors and emoji icons.
*Tip: If you're a new user, click "Load Defaults" to instantly generate standard categories.*

## 4. Logging Transactions
To add an income or expense:
1. Click the blue **"+ Add Transaction"** button in the top right corner.
2. Select whether it is an **Income** or **Expense**.
3. Choose the Account and Category.
4. Enter the amount and an optional description.
5. Click **Save**. Your Dashboard charts and balances will instantly update.

## 5. Budgets
Want to limit how much you spend on "Food"?
1. Click **"Budgets"** on the sidebar.
2. Click **"+ Set Budget"**.
3. Select a category and specify your monthly limit.
The progress bar will show how close you are to reaching your limit based on your logged expenses!

## 6. Reports & Exporting
Need a hard copy of your finances for tax season?
1. Click **"Reports"**.
2. Select your desired date range.
3. Choose either **"Export to PDF"** or **"Export to Excel"**.
Your file will be instantly generated and saved to your computer.

## 7. Settings & Dark Mode
Customize your experience!
- **Dark Mode**: Click the Sun/Moon icon in the top right of the application to switch themes instantly.
- **Settings Panel**: Change your default currency symbol ($, €, £, ₹) or change your account password.
