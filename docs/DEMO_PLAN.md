# Expense Tracker Pro - Demo Video Plan

**Target Length:** 2-3 Minutes
**Format:** 1080p / 60FPS
**Vibe:** Professional, upbeat, showcasing a polished UI.

## Scene 1: The Hook (0:00 - 0:15)
- **Visual:** Fade in from black to the **Login Screen**. Smoothly type in credentials and click "Login".
- **Action:** Transition elegantly into the **Dashboard View (Light Mode)**.
- **Narration Script:** *"Welcome to Expense Tracker Pro—your new personal financial command center. We've built an open-source, desktop-native application that puts you in complete control of your financial data, without the monthly subscriptions."*

## Scene 2: The Dashboard & Insights (0:15 - 0:45)
- **Visual:** Pan across the Dashboard. Highlight the total balance, monthly income, and the dynamic **Financial Health Score** widget.
- **Action:** Hover over the cards to show the micro-interaction borders. Scroll down to show the JFreeChart pie chart and the **AI Insights** box.
- **Narration Script:** *"Your dashboard gives you an instant, high-level overview. Watch your total balance and health score update dynamically. On the right, our built-in Analytics Engine analyzes your spending patterns locally to detect anomalies and offer actionable insights."*

## Scene 3: Adding a Transaction (0:45 - 1:15)
- **Visual:** Click the **"+ Add Transaction"** button in the header. The rounded dialog pops up.
- **Action:** Enter a "$45.00" expense for "Dinner". Use the custom Category Dropdown (showing flags/emojis and colors). Save.
- **Visual:** The Dashboard pie chart instantly animates and updates to reflect the new expense.
- **Narration Script:** *"Logging transactions is frictionless. Choose your account, select a highly customizable category, and hit save. The UI reacts instantly, updating your charts and metrics in real-time."*

## Scene 4: Budgets & Control (1:15 - 1:45)
- **Visual:** Click "Budgets" on the left sidebar. 
- **Action:** Show the budget progress bars filling up. Highlight a budget that is in the "red" (over budget) versus "green".
- **Narration Script:** *"Stay on top of your spending with visual Budgets. Set monthly limits per category and easily see where you're approaching your cap. The progress bars color-code automatically to keep you warned."*

## Scene 5: Customization & Dark Mode (1:45 - 2:15)
- **Visual:** Navigate to "Settings". Show the beautifully styled Currency Dropdown. 
- **Action:** Click the **"🌙 Dark Mode"** toggle in the header. The entire app seamlessly switches to a deep, premium dark theme. Click through Dashboard, Reports, and Categories to show the dark mode consistency.
- **Narration Script:** *"Make it yours. Adjust default currencies, manage security, and switch to our stunning Dark Mode—powered by FlatLaf—for those late-night accounting sessions."*

## Scene 6: Export & Outro (2:15 - 2:30)
- **Visual:** Go to "Reports". Click the "Export to PDF" button. Show a brief flash of the generated PDF invoice.
- **Action:** Return to Dashboard. Fade out with the project logo and GitHub link on screen.
- **Narration Script:** *"When tax season comes, export your data to PDF or Excel with a single click. Expense Tracker Pro—professional financial tracking, completely free and open-source. Check us out on GitHub today."*
